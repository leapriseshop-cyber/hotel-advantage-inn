FAQ Desk

Rooms & Suites FAQs

Common questions regarding our luxury accommodations and services in Civil Lines.

What room categories are available at Hotel Advantage Inn Prayagraj?

We offer three elegantly designed luxury room tiers:
  • Deluxe Room: Entry-tier smart double comfort (King Bed, 130 sq ft).
  • Executive Room: Elite business layout with coffee/kettle setup and organic bath cosmetics (190 sq ft).
  • Super Deluxe Room: Ideal for pilgrim families with a King bed and expandable Sofa lounge (160 sq ft).

Do all rooms at Hotel Advantage Inn include air conditioning and geysers?

Yes, all our rooms feature individually controlled air conditioning, high-speed optical fiber Wi-Fi, flat-screen smart TVs, and pristine Western sanitary bathrooms with electric geysers supplying instant hot water.

What are the standard check-in and check-out times at Hotel Advantage Inn?

Standard check-in is at 12:00 PM and check-out is at 11:00 AM. Direct bookings made on our official website unlock early check-in and late check-out privileges, subject to availability.

Is room service and in-room dining available for guests?

Yes, 24/7 room service and in-room dining from our in-house Golden Leaf Restaurant (serving pure vegetarian Mughlai, Awadhi, and multi-cuisine dishes) are available for all room guests.
