## 2. 180-Day Domination Calendar & Execution Plan

### 2.1 Month 1-2: Core Redesign & Technical SEO Alignment
*   **Target Sprints**:
    *   *Week 1-2*: Implement visual polish to the banquet inquiry box, mobile bottom nav, mobile drawer, and checkout modal in `styles.css`.
    *   *Week 3-4*: Remove inline dark-mode styles from `index.html`. Optimize image assets to WebP format. Ensure mobile text sizes never drop below `13px`.
    *   *Week 5-6*: Inject comprehensive geocoded JSON-LD schemas (Hotel, FoodEstablishment, FAQPage) in the HTML header.
    *   *Week 7-8*: Audit and fix all navigation internal links. Ensure all anchors use localized search keywords.
*   **Targets & KPIs**:
    *   Google PageSpeed scores > 90 on both desktop and mobile viewports.
    *   Zero console errors and 100% correct, validated schema markup.

### 2.2 Month 3-4: AEO, GEO, and Content Domination
*   **Target Sprints**:
    *   *Week 9-10*: Set up the post-checkout WhatsApp review funnel and place the front-desk QR acrylic review stands.
    *   *Week 11-12*: Publish the first 20 local travel and transport guides to establish topical authority clusters.
    *   *Week 13-14*: Configure all Q&A FAQ blocks on programmatic pages. Optimize for Featured Snippets and People Also Ask (PAA).
    *   *Week 15-16*: Align the Google Business Profile (GBP) descriptions, categories, product catalogs, and services.
*   **Targets & KPIs**:
    *   Google Maps local ranking in the top 3 spots (Local 3-Pack) for `hotel in civil lines`.
    *   20% increase in generative engine citation weight across Gemini and Perplexity.

### 2.3 Month 5-6: Programmatic SEO & High-Authority PR Link Outreach
*   **Target Sprints**:
    *   *Week 17-18*: Deploy programmatic landing pages (Junction, High Court, Sangam, University).
    *   *Week 19-20*: Initiate local PR outreach to secure backlinks from Uttar Pradesh tourism directories and regional digital publications.
    *   *Week 21-22*: Partner with regional wedding planners and corporate event management agencies for high-authority referrals.
    *   *Week 23-24*: Run continuous booking funnel audits. Optimize checkout CTA placements and resolve drop-off points.
*   **Targets & KPIs**:
    *   Generate 100+ verified five-star Google Maps reviews.
    *   Capture top-3 organic rankings for targeted local search terms.
    *   Achieve a 25% increase in direct, non-OTA bookings.
