Sacred Martyrdom Site

Chandra Shekhar Azad Park

Alfred Park & Freedom Legacy

Chandrashekhar Azad Park (historically known as Alfred Park) is the largest walled public park in Prayagraj. Covering 133 acres, it is a magnificent green lung containing the beautiful neoclassical Thornhill Mayne Memorial Library, a colossal statue of Azad, and the sacred tree platform where freedom fighter Chandrashekhar Azad attained martyrdom in 1931.
